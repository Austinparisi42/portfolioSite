Latest Projects

Mavens & Mavericks

A mentor/mentee social media platform to help growing developers find guidance.

In progress

This is my most recent work. It is the final project for the Full Stack program I recently attended. Our website serves as a place for developers to sign up as either a mentor or a mentee and matches people seeking mentorship with developers above their skill level. Users will be able to request connections with other users allowing them to message eachother, post questions that relevant developers will be able to see, and further their skills in a more personalized environment than that of other online sources of code knowledge.

At the time of my writing this we are still wrapping up classes, and we are currently working on this project. Although unfinished we plan on continuing the developement of this site long after the course has come to a close.

Check us out on Github!

IceBreakr - A Dating App for the Real World

Our dating app sets out to find it's place in the impersonal dating world of today by using technology to nudge people back into face to face meetings. When a user is out on the town and ready to strike up a conversation with a stranger, they are matched with nearby users based on "ice breakers" and prompted to meet in a mutual location for a genuine first encounter without having to work up the courage to talk to that cute stranger at the end of the bar. This was another class project, and our first attempt at social media. It is unfinished as well, but we have future plans to further develop the app, although Mavens & Mavericks is taking priority.

Dota2 Hero Database

This project is a character database for the popular competitive online game Dota2. The game's 100+ characters are accessed on the Dota2 API and the front page is populated by the hero icons. When you select a hero more information is displayed on that character as well as game play guide videos which are simultaneously searched for with the Youtube API.
